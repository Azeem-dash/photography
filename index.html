About

A developer with
a camera roll

I’m Muhammad Azeem, a software engineer based in Lahore. Photography started as an escape from the screen and turned into the way I pay attention to things — a ring on a wall, a truck against the sun, the road to the northern valleys.

Almost every frame here was taken on a phone. That’s rather the point: you don’t need expensive glass to make an honest photograph, only patience and a feel for the light.
